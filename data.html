Data Removal

How to Remove Your Data

Last updated: February 28, 2026

If you have closed your FaultNet account and wish to have your personal data removed from our systems, please follow the steps below:

  • After closing your account, contact our support team at support@faultnet.io to request data removal.
  • Include the email address associated with your account and specify your request for data deletion.
  • Our team will verify your request and confirm when your data has been removed.

For your security, we may require additional information to verify your identity before processing your request.

Why This Step Is Needed

To protect your privacy and prevent unauthorized data removal, we require direct contact after account closure. This ensures only the rightful account owner can request deletion of their data.

Contact Support

If you have any questions or need assistance, please email support@faultnet.io.
